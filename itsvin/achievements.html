Certifications

Over the last few years I have acquired many certifications. These achievements prove my ability to reach milestones and achieve progress in career goals. I hold many different certifications in IT fields ranging from: hardware and software support, IT processes, project management, and web design.

The CompTIA A+ is the preferred qualifying credential for technical support and IT operational roles.

The CompTIA Network+ helps develop a career in IT infrastructure covering troubleshooting, configuring, and managing networks.

CompTIA Security+ is a global certification that validates the baseline skills you need to perform core security functions.

CompTIA Linux+ validates the competencies required of an early career system administrator supporting Linux systems.

The CompTIA Project+ gives business professionals – inside and outside of IT – the basic concepts to successfully manage small- to medium-sized projects.

The combination of CompTIA certifications create "Stackable Certifications". These stackable certs grant me several titles in the Cybersecurity and Infrastructure areas of IT:

The combination of the A+, Network+, and Security+ have earned me the title of

'Secure Infrastructure Specialist'

The combination of the A+ and Network+ have earned me the title of


'IT Operations Specialist'

The combination of the A+ and the Linux+ have earned me the title of


'Systems Support Specialist'

The combination of the Network+ and the Linux+ have earned me the title of

'Linux Network Professional'

My other certifications revolve around Linux Administration, IT Service Management, and the skills needed to create, support, and administer websites.

The LPIC-1 validates the candidate's ability to perform maintenance tasks on the command line, install and configure a computer running Linux and configure basic networking.

The ITIL Foundation validates awareness of the key elements, concepts and terminology used in the ITIL service lifecycle.

The CIW Site Development Associate certification validates essential web page development skills. The certification tests on developing web sites using HTML5 and CSS3.

The CIW User Interface Designer certification validates the strategies and tactics necessary to design user interfaces, with particular emphasis on creating user interfaces for mobile devices.

The CIW Advanced HTML5 and CSS3 Specialist certification tests essential HTML5, Cascading Style Sheets (CSS) and JavaScript knowledge.
